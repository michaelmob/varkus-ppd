DMCA Notice of Alleged Infringement ("Notice")

  • Identify the copyrighted work(s) that you claim have been infringed.
  • Include where the infringing work(s) is located at.
  • Provide your valid mailing address, telephone number, and e-mail address.

    • "I hereby state that I have a good faith belief that the disputed use of the copyrighted material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law (e.g., as a fair use)."

Submit this Notice, with all items completed, to our abuse report page.
